## Smart Billing & Inventory Management for Retail Businesses

ezPOS is a powerful desktop-based Point of Sale (POS) and Inventory Management solution developed by Thalir Technologies. It helps retail and Holesale businesses streamline billing, manage inventory, track sales, and improve operational efficiency.

### Suitable For

* Supermarkets
* Automobile Spare Parts Stores
* Pathrakadai (Utensil Stores)
* Grocery Stores
* General Retail Shops
* Garmnet Shop

---

## Key Features

### Billing & Sales

* GST Billing
* Tax Invoice Printing
* Barcode & QR Code Billing
* Fast Sales Entry
* Sales Return Management

### Inventory Management

* Real-Time Stock Tracking
* Opening Stock Management
* Purchase Entry
* Stock Adjustment
* Low Stock Monitoring

### Customer Management

* Customer Ledger
* Credit Sales Tracking
* Outstanding Reports
* Customer Purchase History

### Supplier Management

* Supplier Ledger
* Purchase Tracking
* Outstanding Payables

### Reports

* Daily Sales Report
* Profit & Loss Report
* Stock Report
* Purchase Report
* GST Reports
* Customer Outstanding Report

### Security

* User Login Management
* User Role Permissions
* Database Backup & Restore
